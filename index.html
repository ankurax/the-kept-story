THE KEPT STORY

This story was written for someone special.

To The Girl,

Who unknowingly changed The direction of my life.

Happy Birthday, Manisha.

I have made something for us.

Before 7 December 2025,

you and I

were complete strangers.
You lived your life.

I lived mine.

We never knew
the other's existence.
For a long time,

somewhere in my heart,

I carried a quiet hope.
That when I finally visited

Mahabhairav,

I would find a direction
for my future.
On the morning

of 7 December 2025,

before I even reached
the temple...
I came across
a Facebook profile - Yours.

A photograph from Mahabhairav.
A friend request.
A few simple messages.
A few hours later,

while passing the same temple,
you silently prayed

for guidance,
for your future,
for the man
who would walk beside you.
A prayer from one side.

A hope from the other.
Neither of us knew

what the other had wished for.

Yet our paths
had already crossed.
Some call it coincidence.

We call it Mahadev.

🙏
Facebook.

Instagram.

Phone Calls.

Memories.
If someone had told me
on 6 December 2025
that a simple girl from Bedeti

would become

the most important person
in my life...
... That one day

I would share my joys,
my worries,
my dreams,
and my future with her...

I would have laughed..
But, here we are.

Two souls
who found each other
in the vastness of the world.

Two hearts
that beat in harmony
despite the distance.

On 7 December 2025,

I realized that
life has a way of surprising us.

And that
The most beautiful stories
are often the ones
We never saw coming.

By the grace of Mahadev,
